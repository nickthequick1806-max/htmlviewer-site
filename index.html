Privacy Policy

        
How HTML Viewer handles browser data and submissions.

      

      
    

    

      
Last updated: July 10, 2026


      
1. Information stored in your browser

      
HTML Viewer uses browser storage for editor drafts, file names, saved versions, projects, favorites, import history, appearance, preview preferences, and notification settings. This information normally stays on the device and browser profile where it was created.


      
2. Code you enter or import

      
Code entered in the main editor is used to provide editing and preview features. Imported code history is stored locally unless you intentionally use a sharing, community, contact, or connected AI feature.


      
3. Information you submit

      
Contact form submissions send the entered name and message to a configured Discord webhook. Community preset submissions send the provided preset details to Discord. Discord may process and retain that information under its own privacy policy.


      
4. Analytics and service information

      
The service may request aggregated website analytics such as visitor counts, view totals, and general geographic source. Technical providers may receive standard connection information such as IP address, browser type, and request time.


      
5. Third-party features

      
AI, image generation, status, analytics, fonts, icons, and other external features may contact third-party services when used. Community preset submission sends the supplied details and a text-document attachment containing the full preset link to Discord. Do not send secrets or sensitive personal data to those features.


      
6. Retention and deletion

      
You can remove local history cards, saved versions, projects, and other stored information through the interface or by clearing site data in your browser. Information already submitted to a third party may be subject to that provider’s retention controls.


      
7. Security

      
Reasonable safeguards are used, but no browser storage or internet transmission is completely secure. Avoid storing passwords, private keys, access tokens, or confidential production data in the editor.


      
8. Children’s privacy

      
HTML Viewer is not directed to children under 13, and the service should not be used to submit a child’s personal information.


      
9. Your choices

      
You can disable notifications, sounds, or live preview, choose an appearance, delete local records, and choose whether to submit information through connected features.


      
10. Policy updates and contact

      
This policy may change as features evolve. Questions can be sent through the Contact Us form in the page menu.
